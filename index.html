Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically, a basic website takes 3-5 business days, while more complex projects may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide you with a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage my website?

Not at all! We build user-friendly websites and provide complete training. You'll be able to make basic updates yourself, and we're always here to help with more complex changes.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive, meaning they look great and function perfectly on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

What happens after the website is launched?

We provide ongoing support based on your chosen package. This includes technical assistance, minor updates, and guidance on managing your website effectively.

Can I update the content on my website later?

Yes! We can build your website with a content management system that allows you to easily update text, images, and other content. We'll train you on how to use it.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes, we can help you with domain registration and hosting setup. We'll guide you through the entire process and recommend the best options for your needs and budget.
